Kal Ho Naa Ho (2003)

A heartwarming drama centered around Naina, a serious young woman, whose life changes when she meets Aman, a cheerful and spirited man. As Naina falls for Aman, she learns he harbors a painful secret. The film beautifully portrays the complexities of love, friendship, and the importance of living life to the fullest.

Happy New Year (2014)

A heist comedy-drama featuring a group of unlikely thieves, led by Charlie (Shah Rukh Khan), who plan to pull off an elaborate robbery during an international dance competition. Filled with action, humor, and entertaining dance sequences, the film showcases themes of revenge, friendship, and redemption.
